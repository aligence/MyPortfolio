NatureWork

How might we design an application to encourage working professionals in the US to explore green spaces?

Alphabet Soup has identified that Americans are spending less time in nature. While there are various reasons for this problem, a prevalent one is work. So, we wondered if we could design an app that could encourage or make it easier for people who work to get out in nature or their closest green spaces. This is because spending time outdoors is linked to increases in many aspects of our health, both physically and mentally, so this app is aimed to increase the wellness of its users who ordinarily would have struggled to set aside time to spend in nature.
